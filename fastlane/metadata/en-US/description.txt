Do you have a big camera roll? Do you have trouble finding that specific photo you wanna show your friend? Picfind offers you a diverse search engine to explore and search your big camera roll. 

You can search by:

- Location
- Period
- Number Of Faces
- Luminosity
- Album
- Favorites
- Name

By using this filters you can limit the results and find whatever you want.
